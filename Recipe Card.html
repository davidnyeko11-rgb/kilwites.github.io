IMMAGE OF THE DISH

Malakwang, a traditional Ugandan dish, is a flavorful and tangy stew from Northern Uganda. This dish features a unique blend of ingredients, including sweet potatoes, millet bread, groundnut paste, and various spices. Its distinctive taste and cultural significance make it a must-try for anyone interested in exploring Uganda, especially Acoli Land.

Ingredients For Making Malakwang Dish

  • Sweet potatoes

  • Millet bread

  • Groundnut paste

  • Tomatoes

  • Local Salt (Kado Atona)

Procedual Steeps for Preparation of  Malakwang

  1. Get or Pluck the leaves off the bundle
  2.  Wash the leaves thoroughly with clean water
  3. Boil these in water for about 5 to 10 minutes
  4. Add 2 serving spoons local  Salt (Kado Atona) to taste Water - about 250ml
  5. Add Some tomatoes to improve on the flavour (Optional)
  6. Add enough quantity of Odii (Peanut butter or Simsim paste or Groundnut paste)
  7. Add table salt to improve on the taste then mix well.
  8. I assmumed that you had earlier boiled your potatoes or Kwon Kal
  9. Serve the Dish to your guest(s) and enjoy with them - "Dek Malakwang"
